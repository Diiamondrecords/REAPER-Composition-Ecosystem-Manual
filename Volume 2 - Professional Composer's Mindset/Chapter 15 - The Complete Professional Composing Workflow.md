Professional Workflow Principles

Never compose without purpose.

Never solve two unrelated problems simultaneously.

Always finish drafts.

Evaluate after creating.

Develop ideas before replacing them.

Think emotionally before technically.

Composer's Exercise

Choose one unfinished composition.

Do not change anything yet.

Instead, walk through every stage of the professional workflow.

Ask:

Did I skip any step?

Where did uncertainty first appear?

Which stage deserves more attention?

You'll often discover that the real problem appeared much earlier than expected.
