Chapter 13 — Building Your Lifetime Composition System

"The ultimate goal is not to finish one great composition. It is to build a system that allows you to create great music for the rest of your life."

Introduction

By now, your REAPER Composition Ecosystem has become far more than a collection of scripts.

It has become a complete creative methodology.

Professional composers eventually stop asking:

"How do I write this piece?"

Instead they ask:

"How do I build a creative system that consistently produces my best work?"

This chapter focuses on designing that lifelong system.
