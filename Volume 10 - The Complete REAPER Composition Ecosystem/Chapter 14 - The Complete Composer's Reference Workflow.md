Professional Workflow Checklist

Before beginning every new project ask:

✓ Do I know the emotional goal?

✓ Have I designed the structure?

✓ Is the harmonic language established?

✓ Does the melody have identity?

✓ Are variations meaningful?

✓ Is the bass supporting the groove?

✓ Is instrumentation emotionally appropriate?

✓ Does orchestration strengthen the story?

✓ Is production serving the music?

✓ Have I archived everything for future use?
