Appendix C — Complete Genre Orchestration Recipes

"Every musical genre has its own orchestration language. Learning that language allows you to compose authentically while still developing your own artistic voice."

Introduction

This appendix serves as a practical orchestration guide for the most common musical genres.

Rather than rigid rules,

consider these as professional starting points.

Once mastered,

they can be combined into entirely new hybrid styles.

Professional Genre Checklist

Before composing ask:

✓ What emotion defines this genre?

✓ Which instruments are essential?

✓ What harmonic language is expected?

✓ What rhythmic vocabulary belongs here?

✓ How can I remain authentic while developing my own style?

"A genre is not a limitation. It is a musical language. Learn the language first, then tell your own story with it."
