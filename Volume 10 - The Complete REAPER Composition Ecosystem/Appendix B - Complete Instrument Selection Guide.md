Appendix B — Complete Instrument Selection Guide

"One of the fastest ways to improve orchestration is knowing which instrument naturally expresses a particular emotion."

Introduction

This appendix serves as a quick-reference guide for selecting instruments based upon emotional intent rather than habit.

Professional orchestrators rarely ask:

"What instrument should play this?"

Instead they ask:

"Which instrument naturally communicates this emotion?"

Professional Instrument Selection Checklist

Before assigning an instrument ask:

✓ Does this instrument naturally express the intended emotion?

✓ Is it playing within its strongest register?

✓ Does it blend well with surrounding instruments?

✓ Will it support the melody rather than compete?

✓ Would another instrument communicate the story more honestly?

"Professional orchestrators choose instruments the way great directors choose actors—not because they can perform the role, but because they were born for it."
