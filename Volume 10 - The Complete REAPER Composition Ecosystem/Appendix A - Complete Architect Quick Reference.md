The Six Golden Questions

Whenever you become stuck, ask:

What emotion am I trying to communicate?
Which Architect solves my current problem?
Am I composing or editing?
Does this improve the story?
Can this be simpler?
What should the audience remember?

These six questions solve most creative problems.

Final Philosophy

The REAPER Composition Ecosystem exists for one reason:

To remove technical obstacles so that creativity remains at the center of every composition.

Every Architect,

every workflow,

every template,

and every future improvement should support that single goal.

"Technology is most successful when the composer eventually forgets it exists and thinks only about the music."
