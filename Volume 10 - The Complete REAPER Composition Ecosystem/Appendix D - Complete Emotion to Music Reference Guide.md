Introduction

Professional composers rarely begin with harmony.

They begin with emotion.

This appendix serves as a practical reference for translating emotions into musical decisions.

Whenever you ask:

"How should this feel?"

this guide provides a starting point.

Professional Emotion Checklist

Before writing music ask:

✓ What should the audience feel?

✓ Which instruments naturally express that feeling?

✓ Which harmony reinforces it?

✓ Should the music move upward or downward?

✓ Should the dynamics grow or remain intimate?

✓ Would removing notes strengthen the emotion?

"Emotion is the destination. Melody, harmony, rhythm, and orchestration are simply the roads that lead the listener there."
